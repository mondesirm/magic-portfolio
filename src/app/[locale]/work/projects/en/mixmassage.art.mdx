Digitalization of a massage and wellness company including trademark registration, website launch, massage parlor renovation and improved online visibility.

## Description

[Mix Massage Art](https://mixmassage.art) is a company specializing in massage and wellness services, located in Drancy (93700). Their website was designed to offer a soothing and professional user experience, highlighting their diverse services and expertise in the field of massage.

## Objectives

- **Branding**: Create a consistent visual identity that reflects the values of relaxation and wellness of Mix Massage Art.
- **Website**: Design an intuitive website to showcase services and simplify online appointment booking.
- **Parlor**: Create a welcoming and relaxing massage space for clients.
- **Visibility**: Strengthen the company's online presence on social media and wellness platforms.

## Technologies

- **Figma**: Creation of graphic resources.
- **WordPress**: Content management system (fast and easy to manage).
- **Twilio**: Professional phone number, website integration and WhatsApp.

## Results

1. Registered trademark
2. Website launched
3. Business card designed
4. Massage parlor renovated
5. Social media accounts created
